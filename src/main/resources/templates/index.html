Welcome to IRCTC

The Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation (IRCTC) is a government-owned company under the Ministry of Railways, India. Established in 1999, IRCTC plays a pivotal role in managing various services related to Indian Railways, catering to the needs of millions of passengers and tourists across the country.

One of IRCTC's primary functions is facilitating online ticket booking for train journeys, offering a convenient platform for passengers to book tickets, check train schedules, and manage their reservations.IRCTC has streamlined the ticketing process, enabling passengers to book tickets from the comfort of their homes or on the go.

In addition to ticketing, IRCTC manages catering services onboard trains and at railway stations. It ensures the provision of quality food and beverages to passengers during their journey, offering a diverse range of menu options to cater to different tastes and preferences. IRCTC also operates e-catering services, allowing passengers to order food from select restaurants and have it delivered to their train seat.

Through its relentless commitment to customer satisfaction and innovation, IRCTC continues to play a significant role in enhancing the travel experience of passengers, promoting tourism, and contributing to the growth and development of the Indian Railways ecosystem.
